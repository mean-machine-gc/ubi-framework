Ubi. is a framework for understanding and building complex information processing systems. It provides a theoretical foundation grounded in decision theory and bounded rationality, and a practical toolkit for AI-assisted software delivery.

This is a work in progress. The theory is being committed to writing. The tools are in active development. The ideas are being tested on real projects.

The intent is to find the common root that unifies disciplines — business analysis, software architecture, development, quality assurance — that share a fundamental structure but have forgotten a common language. And to build tools that make that structure executable.
