---

Ubi. is created and maintained by Giovanni Chiodi.

The framework is primarily inspired by the work of Alberto Brandolini, Scott Wlaschin, and Herbert Simon. It bridges the gaps between business analysis, software architecture, and AI-assisted development by providing a fundamental model grounded in decision theory and bounded rationality.

The toolkit is open source and available on npm.
